3 Facts about butterflies you maybe never knew

Taste with their Feet:

Butterflies have chemoreceptors on their legs (tarsi) that allow them to "taste" surfaces, helping them find food and host plants for their eggs.

Spectacular Vision:

Their large compound eyes have thousands of lenses and can see colors, including ultraviolet light, which humans cannot, helping them spot flowers and mates.

Amazing Transformation:

They go through four distinct life stages: egg, larva (caterpillar), pupa (chrysalis), and adult, a process called complete metamorphosis, where their body is completely rebuilt.
